— chapter 03 · travel

A week in the Maldives

Various underwater creatures, one overwater bungalow and more shades of blue than I believed existed.
A small travel diary from August 2025.

Malé Atoll,

aka paradise.

It took us almost a whole day to get to the Maldives – safe to say it was worth every second. I expected crystal clear water and beautiful views, but photos really don't do it justice. The colors, the stillness and the feeling of being surrounded by the ocean in every direction were overwhelming in the best way possible.

Something I still think about is our overwater bungalow that had direct access to the ocean. Waking up to that every morning genuinely felt unreal. But for me, the most memorable moment of the whole trip was the snorkeling. I got to swim with sharks (not just once), stingrays, turtles and so many unique fish I had never seen before. This experience was so special to me that I already know I'll be going back.

Where

Centara Ras Fushi Resort, Malé Atoll

When

August 2025

Weather

30°C and sunny

Highlight

A swim with sharks, stingrays and turtles
